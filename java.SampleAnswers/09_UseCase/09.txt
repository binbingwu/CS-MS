
use case name:

Fuel Car

participating actors:

Customer

goal:

To dispense fuel into a car

trigger:

Customer lifts nozzle

precondition:

Payment pre-authorized

postcondition:

Fuel dispensed

basic flow:

Customer removes gas cap, lifts nozzle, and inserts into car.
Pump prompts for fuel type.
Customer chooses fuel type, and starts dispensing fuel.
Pump displays cumulative fuel amount and charge continuously.
Pump prompts for whether to print receipt.
Customer chooses whether to print receipt.
Customer stops dispensing fuel, returns nozzle to pump, and caps fuel tank.
Pump prints receipt if desired.
Customer takes receipt if desired.
