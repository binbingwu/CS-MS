Extreme Programming

In extreme programming, one practice is refactoring, improving the software design to make it amenable to accepting new requirements, thus achieving the principle.

Another practice is continuous integration, which supports small and frequent releases for gaining customer feedback, thus becoming more welcome to new and change requirements.


Scrum

In scrum, one practice is the regular sprint review meeting, where the customer can see their planned and prioritized user stories being satisfied in a working software prototype, thus achieving the principle.

Another practice is the sprint planning meeting, where the customer can help prioritize user stories so that the most valuable requirements are done early.
